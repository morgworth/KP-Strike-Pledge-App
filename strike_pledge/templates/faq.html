FAQ

When is the strike?

The strike will take place on or after Oct 1, 2019.

Will my name or email address be made public?

No. Your pledge will be used to increment the counter on the home page and your comment will be tweeted anonymously by @kaiserstrike19.

Can I be disciplined for using this website?

The National Labor Relations Act protects concerted activities for mutual aid or protection, but we recommend against using this website on paid work time.

Who made this website?

This website was made by the Tech Workers Coalition (TWC) at the request of, and with input from, Kaiser employees. TWC is a coalition of tech workers, labor organizers, community organizers, and friends working in solidarity with existing movements towards social justice, workers' rights, and economic inclusion. For more info about TWC, click here.

What if I have another question?

Click on the Messenger button at the bottom-right of the screen, and send us a message.
