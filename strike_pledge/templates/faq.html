When is the strike?

The strike will take place on or after Oct 1, 2019. The exact date has yet to be determined.

Can I join the strike?

Yes. All Kaiser employees can join the strike. Employees that don't belong to participating Coalition unions must notify their supervisors at least 10 days before the strike.

Can I be disciplined for going on strike?

No, provided you or your union have notified Kaiser at least 10 days before the strike.

Can I be disciplined for using this website?

The National Labor Relations Act protects concerted activities for mutual aid or protection, but we recommend against using this website on paid work time.

Will any of my personal information be shared, or made public?

No. Your pledge will be used to increase the counter on the home page and your comment will be tweeted anonymously by @kaiserstrike19.

Who made this website?

This website was made by the Tech Workers Coalition (TWC) at the request of, and with input from, Kaiser employees. TWC is a coalition of tech workers, labor organizers, community organizers, and friends working in solidarity with existing movements towards social justice, workers' rights, and economic inclusion.
