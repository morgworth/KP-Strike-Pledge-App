Help ensure Kaiser remains a best place to work, and to receive care


Members of the Coalition of Kaiser Permanente Unions are planning a 5-day Unfair Labor Practice (ULP) strike in Oct/Nov 2019. The strike has been called to protest Kaiser's refusal (for 12+ months) to meet and bargain with the Coalition, for which Kaiser was indicted by the National Labor Relations Board (NLRB), and for which Kaiser will stand trial in August 2019. Together, the Coalition unions represent ~85,000 Kaiser employees.
