Be a part of history. Join the largest Kaiser strike ever.


Healthcare union SEIU-UHW is planning a 5-day strike in Oct/Nov 2019. Originally, the strike was planned to protest Kaiser's refusal (for 12+ months) to meet and bargain with the Coalition of KP Unions, which represents ~85,000 Kaiser employees. On December 28, 2018, the National Labor Relations Board (NLRB) issued a formal complaint against Kaiser for refusing to meet and bargain with the Coalition. The NLRB set a trial date of March 19, 2019, but Kaiser's trial was postponed until August 2019, at Kaiser's request.



Tech Workers Coalition
