Common Questions

When is the strike?
The exact date is TBD, but it won't happen until Oct 1 at the earliest.


Will the strike work?
It's worked before and it will work again if we stand together!


Can I join the strike?
All Kaiser employees including doctors, nurses, and non-union staff can join. If you don't belong to a Coalition Union, you must notify your supervisor at least 10 days before the strike.


Can I be disciplined for going on strike?
No, provided that you've notified your manager at least 10 days before the strike (if you don't belong to a Coalition Union). If you belong to a Coalition Union, you don't have to notify your manager.


Can I be disciplined for using this website, or my Kaiser email?
No. The National Labor Relations Act protects concerted action for mutual aid or protection. However, we recommend against using this website on paid work time.


Will my personal information be shared, or made public?
No. Your RSVP will be used to increase the counter above, and your comment will be tweeted anonymously.


Who made this website?
Kaiser employees worked with the Tech Workers Coalition to build this site. TWC is a coalition of tech workers, labor organizers, community organizers, and friends working in solidarity with existing movements towards social justice, workers' rights, and economic inclusion. Learn more about TWC.
