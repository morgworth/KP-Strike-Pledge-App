Common Questions


Who made this website?
The Tech Workers Coalition (TWC) made this website at the request of Kaiser employees. TWC is a coalition of tech workers, labor organizers, community organizers, and friends working in solidarity with existing movements towards social justice, workers' rights, and economic inclusion. Learn more about TWC.


Can I be disciplined for using this website, or my Kaiser email?
No. The National Labor Relations Act (NLRA) protects concerted action for mutual aid or protection. However, we recommend against using this website on paid work time.


Can I join the strike?
Yes. All Kaiser employees can join, including doctors, nurses, and non-union employees. If you don't belong to a participating Coalition Union, you must notify your supervisor at least 10 days before the strike, preferably in writing.


Can I be disciplined for going on strike?
No, provided that you've notified your manager at least 10 days before the strike (if you don't belong to a participating Coalition Union). If you belong to a participating Coalition Union, you don't have to notify your manager.


Will the strike work?
It's worked before and it will work again if we stand together!


Will my personal information be shared, or made public?
No. Your strike pledge is anonymous and will only increase the counter at the top of this page.


When is the strike, exactly?
The exact date is still tbd.
