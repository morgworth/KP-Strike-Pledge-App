Common Questions

When is the strike?
The exact date is TBD, but it won't happen until Oct 1 at the earliest.


Can I join the strike?
Yes. All Kaiser employees including doctors, nurses, and non-union staff can join. If you don't belong to a participating Coalition Union, you must notify your supervisor at least 10 days before the strike, preferably in writing.


Will the strike work?
It's worked before and it will work again if we stand together!


Can I be disciplined for going on strike?
No, provided that you've notified your manager at least 10 days before the strike (if you don't belong to a participating Coalition Union). If you belong to a participating Coalition Union, you don't have to notify your manager.


Can I be disciplined for using this website, or my Kaiser email?
No. The National Labor Relations Act (NLRA) protects concerted action for mutual aid or protection. However, we recommend against using this website on paid work time.


Will my personal information be shared, or made public?
No. Your strike pledge and tweet will be anonymous.


Who made this website?
The Tech Workers Coalition (TWC) made this website at the request of, and with input from, Kaiser employees. TWC is a coalition of tech workers, labor organizers, community organizers, and friends working in solidarity with existing movements towards social justice, workers' rights, and economic inclusion.
